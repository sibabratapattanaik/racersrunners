Weekday Regular Activities

  • Monday: Rest Day
  • Tuesday: Group run, 10 to 15 km at Ekamra Park. Start Time: 5:30 AM
  • Wednesday: Cycling Race Day. 50/60 km peloton ride with an average speed between 28 km/h and 32 km/h. Start Time: 5:30 AM
    • Start Location: Hotel Crown
    • Route: Hotel Crown to Cuttuct-Puri bypass road, Lingipur, Uttara, Tollgate, and return on the same route.
  • Thursday: Group run, 10 km at Ekamra Park. Start Time: 5:30 AM
  • Friday: Easy fun ride through Chandaka and Nandankanan Road. Overall distance is 50 to 60 km. A social ride.
  • Saturday: Long run (15+ km) or long ride (100 km), based on team decision.
  • Sunday: Long run (15+ km) or long ride (100 km), based on team decision.

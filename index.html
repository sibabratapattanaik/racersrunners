About Us

“Racers & Runners” is a group of sports enthusiasts who believe in leading a healthy lifestyle and most often come together to achieve the same through various sports activities - cycling, running & swimming. The group is guided by a larger mission to encourage the society to adopt sports and constantly upgrade to latest technologies and innovations.
